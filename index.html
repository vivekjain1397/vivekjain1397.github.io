Projects

MindWake - 2014-2015

Technologies used - Arduino, BlueSMiRF Bluetooth software, C, Circuit Maker, 3D Printer

Mindwake is a wearable device which alerts a driver when he/she is losing attention or falling asleep. It utilizes brainwave and other sensor data along with my algorithm to determine if the driver has lost attention or fallen asleep. Data is sent via Bluetooth and interpreted by an Arduino. Provisional Patent pending.




PinLoco - 2015

Technologies used - Swift (iOS), Parse

PinLoco is a location based iOS app which allows users to take pictures at their current location and post to a map. After that, the user can view the pins of other users in that location and rate the picture they took. The purpose of this app is to allow users to create a sort of trail so other users can view that trail and maybe check out similar locations. This app was created at HackPrinceton 2015 and will be continued.



CalGroupFinder - 2015

Technologies used - HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, MySQL

Made at the 2014 Spring PennApps Hackathon (February 14-16, 2014), BetsOn is the easiest, most sophisticated, and most natural way to make bets with your friends. Experience



Research Assistant for 3D robotics to Map an Unknown Area - 2016

At the highest level we are doing mapping with multi-robots. This is one of the two tasks in the Simultaneous Localization and Mapping problem SLAM. The robot teams consist of an observer robot Zumi (camera) and 5-9 picket robots Roaches. The Roaches are fast, cheap but lack computing power and the Zumi is slow, expensive, but has computing power of the smart phone. The Zumi is the leader of each robot team and commands all Roach movements. The roaches role is finding terrain the Zumi can not cross. Based on the orientation and position of the roaches, a map is made of the environment. Roaches will eventually have CO_2 sensors to try to find humans.
